Welcome to blackjack!

Objective

The objective of the game is to obtain as much money as you can before all the cards are played from the deck. When player cash hits 0, the deck has less than 9 cards or player presses cash out, the current game match ends and the amount of cash left is the player's overall score.

How to play
  • Try and get card total as close to 21 without going over
  • Ace is worth 1 and J,Q,K is worth 10
  • Input appropriate bet amount in input box and press bet to place bet
  • Deal places 2 cards for both dealer and player
  • Hit draws an addtional card
  • Stand holds on to the player's current hand and compares it with dealer's
  • Cash out ends the match and the current cash is used as the overall score
  • If tie then player reclaims the cash betted
  • If both sides are busted then dealer wins
